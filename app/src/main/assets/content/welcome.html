MapMint4ME is easy!


  1. Import your data

    First of all, you should configure a MapMint server from which you will be able to download a local copy of the database for offline edition.

  2. Edit the data on the field

    With or without internet connection, go on the field and record the data you need.

  3. Export your data

    At the end of your mission or once you get internet connection back, simply press the export button from the Export module to get your data uploaded on the MapMint server!
